FAQs

If you cannot find an answer on this page, be sure to contact us. We will respond asap, during business hours.

Shipping

Can I ship to a different address than my billing address?

Yes, and for quick checkout, up to three addresses can be stored in your SBM online account.

What shipping options do you offer?

Flat Rate Any Size USA for $3.95. We also offer USPS, UPS and FedEx options.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. International Priority Airmail is reliable and cost effective. We also offer USPS, UPS and FedEx.

Can you ship as a gift?

No, we cannot mark international packages as gifts or lower values.

How do I check the shipping fee?

Place items in the cart and then visit the cart. There is a shipping estimator button available to check rates and options.

Where can I learn more?

For full details visit our Shipping page.

Payment

How safe is storing a payment card on your website?

Very safe. Melodic Strings does not store your actual card information, rather creates a unique profile pin which allows our processor to rebill you.
In addition, this pin only works on our website.

What types of payment do you accept?

For full details visit our Payment page.

Back-ordered Products (not in stock)

How do I know if a product is out of stock and will backorder?

In the shopping cart, if there is a back ordered product, you will see (in red letters) quantity in stock and quantity that will back-order.
Upon clicking the Proceed To Checkout button, you'll see a popup window indicating the quantity that will back-order.
On the checkout page, the product summary section will display quantity in stock and quantity that will back-order.
Upon completing your order, the confirmation email will display quantity in stock and quantity that will back-order.
When your first shipment is delivered, the invoice will contain the dollar amount that will ship separately and what new order number back-orders split to.

How long do backorders take to get back in stock?

Some are filled within 24 hours, others a few days and occasionally a few weeks.
In the event of an unusual delay we will contact you at the 2-3 week and 5 week markers to update you on ETA.

Do you charge additional shipping fees for backordered items that ship separately?

No. All backorders ship free.

Coupons & Discounts

Will my coupon work on top of discounted products?

No, it will not combine both. Coupons equal to or less than the discount will not apply.
However, if your coupon is for a greater discount, only the higher discount will apply.

Can I use more than one coupon code during checkout?

Yes, you can use more than one coupon code, as long as they are for different products or uses.
If two different coupons are assigned to the same product, only the higher discount will apply.
