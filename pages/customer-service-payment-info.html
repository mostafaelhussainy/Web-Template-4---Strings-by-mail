Payment Information

Credit Card / Paypal / Amazon Pay

Visa, Master Card, Discover, American Express, JCB, Diner's Club and EnRoute are accepted for online, faxed, phone or postal mail orders. Email orders with card info are not accepted. Credit cards are automatically verified when the order is submitted. The billing address given in the online order form must match the card holder's billing address. Alternate ship-to addresses are allowed. Large orders may require matching bill-to/ship-to addresses.

Saved Payment Profiles with your credit card allows for fast and convenient checkout. We do not store the number itself, rather a secure payment profile id that can only be used on stringsbymail.com.

PayPal caters to more than 200 countries/regions and supports 25 currencies. For a complete list click here. If your country is not supported, PayPal will not appear on the checkout page. However, simply checkout using Other Pay Options and we will send you a PayPal invoice to pay outside of our website.

Amazon Pay for those who wish to pay using payment options found in their Amazon account.

Gift Certificates

Gift Certificates can be purchased here. Gift Certificates (Gift Credit) can be sent in any amount to any valid email via "My Account" when logged in. To use during checkout, there is a Gift Certificate option on the payment page and will only appear if you have a positive balance. You can use all of it, but if using a portion, you must apply the portion and then use another form of payment to complete the purchase.

Other Pay Options

NOTE: Your order will not be shipped and product held until payment is received, excluding approved purchase orders. We will email you to arrange payment. If after two emails without a response, your order will be canceled.

Western Union: Please follow the instructions emailed to you within 24 business hours after your order has been placed.

Money Orders: Please allow 2-3 business days for money orders to clear.

USA Domestic Orders Only

School Purchase Orders: Please reference your PO NUMBER in the comment box during checkout. Once you submit the order, we will contact the email address on file with an update and approval.

Personal Checks: Please allow 2-3 business days for personal checks to clear.
