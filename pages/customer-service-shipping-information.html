Shipping Information

International IPA shipments: Please note there are COVID-19 related shipping delays and suspensions across the world for International USPS ePacket service. Use the Asendia Service Updates page to see if your country is affected. We are shipping International orders, but it may be held at customs until clearance is lifted. Please following the tracking link provided in your shipping email. Other shipping options are available, like FedEx, which are not experiencing delays.

Same Day Shipping
  • Domestic shipping options received by 3:00 pm EST Monday-Friday ship that same day.
  • International USPS ePacket (IPA) selected orders received by 8:00 am EST Tuesday-Friday ship that same day.
  • International shipping options via USPS, Fed Ex and UPS received by 3:00 pm EST Monday-Friday ship that same day.
  • Other orders not defined above will ship by next business day.

Please contact us for time sensitive orders.

Exceptions include:
Holidays which are listed at the bottom of this page. If we need to contact you concerning your order/shipment before shipping and await a response.
Occasional extra high volume sale days and the annual Black Friday period.
If your product is flagged as a backorder in the shopping cart, checkout page and order confirmation email, we will stay in contact with you if unexpected delays occur. Or, if in the rare case our stock levels are inaccurate and we accidentally do not have your product to ship, it will become a backorder. We will notify you ASAP.

All Shipments

Shipping Insurance is included in ALL Melodic Strings shipments.
Occasionally UPS or FedEx may be used for larger, heavier orders.
UPS and FedEx cannot deliver to USPS P.O. Boxes.
While Melodic Strings does not guarantee any delivery at a specific time, location, or date, we do offer UPS and FedEx options with guaranteed timelines. Additionally USPS is extremely reliable.
Melodic Strings quickly and efficiently makes sure each order is delivered to the chosen shipping agency. We are not able to control what happens to packages once they are in the shipping system, however we will always strive to be of assistance to the best of our ability.
Displayed shipping costs include complete postage, packaging and handling.

Domestic Shipping

As part of Melodic Strings's policy of offering value to it's customers, an "Flat Rate Any Size" option of just $3.95 is offered for domestic shipping for any size order. Additional prioritized, faster shipping options are offered during checkout.
Domestic orders shipped via the Flat Rate Any Size shipping option will be delivered via US Postal Service First Class, Priority and occasionally FedEx or UPS.
To provide you best service, SBM never ships via "slower" services like parcel post or media mail.
Delivery times for First Class mail is usually 1 to 5 business days for the continental USA.
Delivery Confirmation is used on all USPS shipments.

International Shipping

Weight and destination determine International shipping prices. We proudly service over 230 countries, via International USPS ePacket (currently IPA), USPS Priority and Express as well as FedEx and UPS services. International USPS ePacket is handled as Priority Mail in the USA and handled as First Class Mail or Priority Mail based on the destination country.
Delivery times: International USPS ePacket averages 5 to 25 days but is not guaranteed. In some cases it can take as long as 60 days. Faster services are available during checkout in as little as 2-3 days delivery after clearing customs. Clearing customs is the #1 reason why a package may take longer than expected.
Tracking: International Express and Priority do include tracking numbers for complete tracking information and provide quicker travel. If shipping International USPS ePacket (currently IPA), our least expensive and most cost effective international shipping method, the following countries will receive a Fully Tracked Service, door to door, included at no extra charge: Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Bulgaria, Canada, Columbia, Croatia, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Georgia, Germany, Gibraltar, Greece, Hong Kong, Hungary, Iceland, India, Indonesia, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Japan, Korea (Republic of), Lebanon, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malaysia, Malta, Mexico, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Serbia, Singapore, Slovakia, Slovenia, South Korea, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey, United Arab Emirates, United Kingdom and Viet Nam.
Customs Duties or other Taxes levied at the destination address are not included in the shipping charges and are the sole responsibility of the customer, with the exception of *Australia and **Norway. We cannot change the amount of invoices, send from an individual, or send as gifts or samples. Potential duties/fees are your responsibility, if your order value exceeds limits for shipments into your country. *Australian customers will be charged 10% tax during checkout. Melodic Strings is registered through AUSid. Our ARN number is 3000 1388 7078. *Norway customers will be charged 25% tax during checkout. Melodic Strings is registered through Skatteetaten. Our VOEC number is 2042878.
You may have to pickup your package at your local post office or customs/duties office.
Packages shown to be refused by recipient or abandoned at customs or postal offices do not qualify for a refund unless returned.
