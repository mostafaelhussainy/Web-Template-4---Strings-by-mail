    Delivery charges are:
  • Up to 30 items: $18 (Instead of $22.5, a discount of 20%)
  • From 31 to 60 items: $24 (Instead of $30 a discount of 20%)

You may wish to consider consolidating your orders to help reduce your cost. You may do so online or call us for assistance.

Most messengers will send you a text message in advance of the delivery to ensure you will be available to personally sign for it.

If you do not accept the first delivery attempt ,the messenger will leave a note with a telephone number for you to reschedule your delivery. We will email you a link to track the status of your order.

The messenger will only deliver to the shipping address on this order, so be sure your address is accurate before placing the order.

We will not be able to change the delivery address manually.
